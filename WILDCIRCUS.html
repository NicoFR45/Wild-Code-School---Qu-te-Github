About company

Since 2012, our company went around the world and attracted thousands of spectators. We offer to our spectators an unforgettable, amazing show. The smallest as the biggest will be conquered after the big evening we promise to you!
We created 3 years ago, big national reserves in the United States, in association with the International Fund for Animal Welfare for that every animal has free right to life.
We make a commitment to offer you the biggest show which is, in the respect on all our convictions and our principles.

About the show

The show begins at 8.PM, if you don't have tickets, please present you 30min early, to be sure to obtain it and have the best place :)!
This show will amaze the smallest as the biggest, and you will be amazed all the time!.

1st part ~ 30min

The tightrope walkers will open this big evening! They will go at top speed to the ground as in the air, Get ready to live a great moment

2nd part ~ 30min

Our illusionist will offer you an amazing show, the most sceptical of you will be convinced!

3rd part ~ 20min

Our clown does not always know how to play the trumpet, but he has a lot of humor. Careful if you see him a cream pie in hands ...

4th part ~ 30min

Our animals are going to amaze you. Lions, tigers, elephants, sea lions and many others!

Ending ~ 20min

Big final: a race of motorcycles in a sphere and one very great surprise...
